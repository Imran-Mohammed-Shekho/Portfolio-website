Gym Workout & Supplement Store

A Flutter app concept that combines workout planning with a supplement storefront. The project is centered on modular screen design, animated transitions, and a dark-mode-friendly fitness aesthetic.

Overview

This project combines two related flows in one Flutter app: workout guidance and supplement purchasing. The structure is aimed at users who want training content and product access in the same mobile experience.

It stands out in the portfolio because it mixes lifestyle content, store features, and animated navigation instead of focusing on only one screen type.

Key Features

Built to merge fitness guidance with product browsing.

Workout routines

Users can move through training plans and exercise guidance inside a focused mobile layout.

Integrated store

Supplements and product bundles can be surfaced without leaving the workout experience.

Smooth transitions

Screen changes and content presentation are designed to feel energetic rather than static.

Modular widgets

Reusable sections make it easier to expand workouts, products, and categories over time.

Dark mode styling

A darker visual system supports the fitness theme and keeps contrast strong on mobile screens.

Commerce-ready flow

The app structure supports future checkout and inventory expansion without redesigning the core.
