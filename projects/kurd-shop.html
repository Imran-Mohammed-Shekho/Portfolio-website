Kurd Shop

A modern shopping app concept built with Flutter. The project focuses on browsing flow, responsive product screens, clean cart interactions, and an interface that feels polished on mobile devices.

Overview

Kurd Shop was designed as a complete e-commerce experience for mobile. The goal was to present products clearly, keep navigation straightforward, and make purchase-related actions feel quick and predictable.

The interface leans on reusable Flutter widgets, image-heavy layouts, product listing cards, and checkout-oriented interactions. It is the strongest commerce-focused piece in this portfolio.
